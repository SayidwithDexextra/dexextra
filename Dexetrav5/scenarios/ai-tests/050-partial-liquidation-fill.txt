# Test 050: Partial Liquidation Fill (Thin Book)
# Liquidation requires 200 ALU but only 50 ALU available on the book.
# Contract treats >= 50% fill as success; verify behavior with 25% fill.
# Verifies: partial liquidation fill handling, remaining amount accounting.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 100
U2 DEP 5000
U3 DEP 5000

# U1 opens leveraged long of 200
U2 LS 2.50 1 200
U1 LB 2.50 1 200

SLEEP 500

# Only post 50 units of liquidity to absorb the liquidation
U3 LB 0.50 1 50

SLEEP 300

# Crash the price
U2 LS 0.50 1 10

SLEEP 500

# Liquidate — only 50/200 available = 25% fill
LD 1

SLEEP 500
