# Test 050: Partial Liquidation Fill (Thin Book)
# Short position is liquidated when only partial liquidity exists on the book.
# The remaining position should be closed via vault fallback.
# Verifies: partial liquidation fill handling, vault fallback for remainder.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 200
U2 DEP 5000
U3 DEP 10000
U4 DEP 10000

# U1 opens a short at $2.50
U1 LS 2.50 1 100
U2 LB 2.50 1 100
SLEEP 500
ASSERT POSITION U1 SHORT == 100

# Pump the price with executed trades
U3 LB 10.00 1 10
U4 LS 10.00 1 10
SLEEP 300
U3 LB 10.00 1 10
U4 LS 10.00 1 10
SLEEP 500

# Place partial liquidity (only 30 asks available to close a 100-lot short)
U4 LS 10.00 1 30
SLEEP 300

# Liquidate — partial fill from book, remainder via vault fallback
LD 1
SLEEP 500
