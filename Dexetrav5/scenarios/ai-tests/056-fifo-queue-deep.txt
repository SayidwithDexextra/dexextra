# Test 056: Deep FIFO Queue at Single Price Level
# 4 users each place 5 orders at the same price → 20 orders at one level.
# Then a large taker sweeps through them all.
# Verifies: FIFO ordering, gas usage for deep queue, correct fill allocation.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 10000
U2 DEP 10000
U3 DEP 10000
U4 DEP 10000

# Stack bids at $2.00 from multiple users
U1 LB 2.00 1 10
U1 LB 2.00 1 10
U1 LB 2.00 1 10
U2 LB 2.00 1 10
U2 LB 2.00 1 10
U2 LB 2.00 1 10
U3 LB 2.00 1 10
U3 LB 2.00 1 10
U3 LB 2.00 1 10
U4 LB 2.00 1 10

SLEEP 500

ASSERT BID == 2.00

# Deployer sells through entire queue (100 units)
@ DEP 50000
@ LS 2.00 1 100

SLEEP 1000

ASSERT POSITION U1 LONG == 30
ASSERT POSITION U2 LONG == 30
ASSERT POSITION U3 LONG == 30
ASSERT POSITION U4 LONG == 10
