# Test 034: Long Position Realizes Loss
# U1 buys at $3.00, price drops, sells at $2.00 → loss.
# Verifies: loss realization, collateral deduction.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 5000
U2 DEP 5000
U3 DEP 5000

# Open long at $3.00
U2 LS 3.00 1 50
U1 LB 3.00 1 50

SLEEP 500
ASSERT POSITION U1 LONG == 50

# Close at $2.00 (loss)
U3 LB 2.00 1 50
U1 LS 2.00 1 50

SLEEP 500
