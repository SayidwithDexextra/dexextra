# Test 055: Very High Price Trading
# Trade at a very high price to stress margin and notional calculations.
# Verifies: high-price arithmetic, no overflow in price * amount.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 500000
U2 DEP 500000

# $1000.00 per ALU
U1 LB 1000.00 1 100
U2 LS 1000.00 1 100

SLEEP 500

ASSERT BOOK_EMPTY
ASSERT POSITION U1 LONG == 100
