# Test 024: Best Bid/Ask Updates After Fill
# After a full fill, the next-best price level should become BBO.
# Verifies: BBO update after level exhaustion.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 10000
U2 DEP 5000
U3 DEP 5000

# Two bid levels
U1 LB 2.00 1 50
U1 LB 2.10 1 50

SLEEP 300
ASSERT BID == 2.10

# Sell into the best bid (2.10) — exhaust it
U2 LS 2.10 1 50

SLEEP 500

# Best bid should now fall to 2.00
ASSERT BID == 2.00
