# Test 031: Short Position Open and Close
# U1 goes short, then closes with a buy.
# Verifies: short opening, short closing, PnL realization.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 5000
U2 DEP 5000
U3 DEP 5000

# Open short: U1 sells to U2
U2 LB 2.50 1 50
U1 LS 2.50 1 50

SLEEP 500
ASSERT POSITION U1 SHORT == 50

# Close short: U1 buys back from U3
U3 LS 2.50 1 50
U1 LB 2.50 1 50

SLEEP 500
