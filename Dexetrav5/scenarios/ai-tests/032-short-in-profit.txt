# Test 032: Short Position Realizes Profit
# U1 shorts at $3.00, price drops to $2.00. U1 buys back at lower price.
# Verifies: profitable short close, realized PnL positive.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 5000
U2 DEP 5000
U3 DEP 5000

# Open short at $3.00
U2 LB 3.00 1 50
U1 LS 3.00 1 50

SLEEP 500
ASSERT POSITION U1 SHORT == 50

# Close at $2.00 (profit for the short)
U3 LS 2.00 1 50
U1 LB 2.00 1 50

SLEEP 500
