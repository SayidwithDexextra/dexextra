# Test 049: Liquidation With Empty Order Book
# Liquidate a user when there is zero liquidity on the opposite side.
# Should trigger direct vault fallback rather than a market order.
# Verifies: no-liquidity liquidation path, direct vault fallback.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 200
U2 DEP 5000
U3 DEP 10000
U4 DEP 10000

# U1 opens a short at $2.50
U1 LS 2.50 1 100
U2 LB 2.50 1 100
SLEEP 500
ASSERT POSITION U1 SHORT == 100

# Pump the price with executed trades
U3 LB 10.00 1 10
U4 LS 10.00 1 10
SLEEP 300
U3 LB 10.00 1 10
U4 LS 10.00 1 10
SLEEP 500

# Book is now empty (no resting orders to close against) — vault fallback kicks in
LD 1
SLEEP 500
