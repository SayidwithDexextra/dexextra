# Test 042: Socialized Loss Scenario
# Short user with minimal collateral gets liquidated when price pumps.
# The excess loss is socialized across profitable users.
# Verifies: socialized loss mechanics, liquidation of deeply underwater short.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 200
U2 DEP 5000
U3 DEP 10000
U4 DEP 10000

# U1 opens a short at $2.50 with small margin
U1 LS 2.50 1 100
U2 LB 2.50 1 100
SLEEP 500
ASSERT POSITION U1 SHORT == 100

# Pump the price — U1's short is deeply underwater
U3 LB 10.00 1 10
U4 LS 10.00 1 10
SLEEP 300
U3 LB 10.00 1 10
U4 LS 10.00 1 10
SLEEP 500

# Liquidate the underwater short
LD 1
SLEEP 500
