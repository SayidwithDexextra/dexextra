# Test 014: Price Improvement
# U1 posts a sell at $2.00. U2 posts a buy at $2.50 (above the ask).
# Should match at the resting order's price ($2.00), giving U2 price improvement.
# Verifies: price-time priority, taker gets best resting price.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 5000
U2 DEP 5000

# Resting ask at $2.00
U1 LS 2.00 1 50

SLEEP 300

# Aggressive buy at $2.50 — should fill at $2.00
U2 LB 2.50 1 50

SLEEP 500

ASSERT BOOK_EMPTY
ASSERT POSITION U2 LONG == 50
ASSERT POSITION U1 SHORT == 50
