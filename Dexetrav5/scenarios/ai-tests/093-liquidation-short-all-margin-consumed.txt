# Test 093: Short Liquidation — All Margin Consumed
# Short user's loss exceeds their entire margin when price pumps.
# Verifies: bad debt handling, socialized loss trigger, margin confiscation.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 200
U2 DEP 10000
U3 DEP 10000
U4 DEP 10000

# U1 opens short at $1.00 with tiny margin
U1 LS 1.00 1 200
U2 LB 1.00 1 200
SLEEP 500
ASSERT POSITION U1 SHORT == 200

# Price spikes to $5.00 — U1's loss far exceeds their margin
U3 LB 5.00 1 10
U4 LS 5.00 1 10
SLEEP 300
U3 LB 5.00 1 10
U4 LS 5.00 1 10
SLEEP 500

# Liquidate U1 — expect bad debt / socialized loss
LD 1
SLEEP 500
