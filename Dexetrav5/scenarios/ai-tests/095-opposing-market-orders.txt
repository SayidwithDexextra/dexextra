# Test 095: Two Opposing Market Orders
# U1 places a market buy, U2 places a market sell. Neither has a resting order.
# If no resting liquidity, both should fail gracefully.
# Verifies: market order without resting liquidity rejection.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 5000
U2 DEP 5000

# Both try market orders — no resting orders on book, should revert
STRICT OFF
U1 MB 1 50
U2 MS 1 50
STRICT ON

SLEEP 500
# Book should still be empty
ASSERT BOOK_EMPTY
