# Test 027: Multi-User Simultaneous Liquidation
# Multiple users open shorts with minimal margin; price pumps → all get liquidated.
# Verifies: sequential liquidation of multiple underwater users.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 200
U2 DEP 200
U3 DEP 5000
U4 DEP 10000

# U1 and U2 open shorts at $2.50
U1 LS 2.50 1 100
U2 LS 2.50 1 100
U3 LB 2.50 1 200
SLEEP 500
ASSERT POSITION U1 SHORT == 100
ASSERT POSITION U2 SHORT == 100

# Pump the price — both shorts are underwater
U3 LB 10.00 1 10
U4 LS 10.00 1 10
SLEEP 300
U3 LB 10.00 1 10
U4 LS 10.00 1 10
SLEEP 500

# Liquidate both
LD 1
LD 2
SLEEP 500
