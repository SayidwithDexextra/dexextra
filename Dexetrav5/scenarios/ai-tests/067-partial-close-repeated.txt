# Test 067: Repeated Partial Close
# Open 100 units long, then close in 5 batches of 20.
# Verifies: partial close accounting, position decrement, PnL realization per tranche.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 10000
U2 DEP 10000
U3 DEP 10000

# Open 100 long
U2 LS 2.50 1 100
U1 LB 2.50 1 100
SLEEP 500
ASSERT POSITION U1 LONG == 100

# Close 20 at a time
U3 LB 2.50 1 20
U1 LS 2.50 1 20
SLEEP 300
ASSERT POSITION U1 LONG == 80

U3 LB 2.50 1 20
U1 LS 2.50 1 20
SLEEP 300
ASSERT POSITION U1 LONG == 60

U3 LB 2.50 1 20
U1 LS 2.50 1 20
SLEEP 300
ASSERT POSITION U1 LONG == 40

U3 LB 2.50 1 20
U1 LS 2.50 1 20
SLEEP 300
ASSERT POSITION U1 LONG == 20

U3 LB 2.50 1 20
U1 LS 2.50 1 20
SLEEP 300
