# Test 019: Margin Top-Up on Open Position
# Open a position, then top up its margin.
# Verifies: top-up mechanics, available collateral adjustment.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 5000
U2 DEP 5000

# Open position
U2 LS 2.50 1 50
U1 LB 2.50 1 50

SLEEP 500
ASSERT POSITION U1 LONG == 50

# Top up position margin by $200
U1 TUP 1 200

SLEEP 300
