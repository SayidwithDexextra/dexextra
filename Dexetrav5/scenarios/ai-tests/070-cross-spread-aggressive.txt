# Test 070: Aggressive Cross-Spread Limit Order
# Place a buy order far above the resting ask — should execute at ask price, not buy price.
# Verifies: price-time priority, taker gets resting maker price.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 10000
U2 DEP 10000

# Resting ask at $1.00
U1 LS 1.00 1 100

SLEEP 300

# Buy at $10.00 — wildly above the ask. Should fill at $1.00.
U2 LB 10.00 1 100

SLEEP 500

ASSERT BOOK_EMPTY
ASSERT POSITION U2 LONG == 100
