# Test 053: High-Precision Price (6-Decimal Boundary)
# Use prices with maximum precision (6 decimals) to test price encoding.
# Verifies: 6-decimal price storage, no truncation, correct matching at precise prices.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 5000
U2 DEP 5000

# Price with full 6-decimal precision: $2.123456
U1 LB 2.123456 1 50
U2 LS 2.123456 1 50

SLEEP 500

ASSERT BOOK_EMPTY
ASSERT POSITION U1 LONG == 50
