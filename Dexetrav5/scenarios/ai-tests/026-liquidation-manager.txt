# Test 026: Liquidation via CoreVault (LD) — Short Side
# Similar to 025 but verifies a second independent short liquidation scenario.
# LMLD path requires marketToOrderBook registration; use LD (CoreVault→OB) instead.
# Verifies: short-side liquidation through CoreVault delegatecall path.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 200
U2 DEP 5000
U3 DEP 10000
U4 DEP 10000

# U1 opens a short at $2.50
U1 LS 2.50 1 100
U2 LB 2.50 1 100
SLEEP 500
ASSERT POSITION U1 SHORT == 100

# Pump the mark by executing trades at $10
U3 LB 10.00 1 10
U4 LS 10.00 1 10
SLEEP 300
U3 LB 10.00 1 10
U4 LS 10.00 1 10
SLEEP 500

# Liquidate U1
LD 1
SLEEP 500
