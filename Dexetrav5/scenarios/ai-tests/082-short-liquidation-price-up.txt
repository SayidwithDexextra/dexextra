# Test 082: Short Liquidation (Price Moves Up)
# U1 opens a short. Price rises sharply via executed trades → U1 liquidated.
# Verifies: short-side liquidation trigger, correct direction detection.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 200
U2 DEP 5000
U3 DEP 10000
U4 DEP 10000

# U1 opens short at $2.00
U1 LS 2.00 1 200
U2 LB 2.00 1 200
SLEEP 500
ASSERT POSITION U1 SHORT == 200

# Price rises via executed trades — bad for shorts
U3 LB 8.00 1 10
U4 LS 8.00 1 10
SLEEP 300
U3 LB 8.00 1 10
U4 LS 8.00 1 10
SLEEP 500

# Liquidate the underwater short
LD 1
SLEEP 500
