# Test 090: Three-Level Ask Sweep With Different Makers
# Each price level has a different maker. Buyer sweeps all three.
# Verifies: multi-maker matching, correct per-maker position allocation.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 5000
U2 DEP 5000
U3 DEP 5000
U4 DEP 20000

U1 LS 2.00 1 30
U2 LS 2.50 1 30
U3 LS 3.00 1 30

SLEEP 300

# U4 buys 90 — fills all 3 levels
U4 LB 3.00 1 90

SLEEP 500

ASSERT BOOK_EMPTY
ASSERT POSITION U1 SHORT == 30
ASSERT POSITION U2 SHORT == 30
ASSERT POSITION U3 SHORT == 30
ASSERT POSITION U4 LONG == 90
