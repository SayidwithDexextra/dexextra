# Test 083: Long Liquidation (Price Moves Down)
# With 100% initial margin, longs are fully collateralized and cannot be liquidated.
# Instead, we test that a SHORT gets liquidated when price moves against it (up).
# This mirrors the long liquidation concept but for the direction that works.
# Verifies: liquidation triggers correctly for the vulnerable side.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 200
U2 DEP 5000
U3 DEP 10000
U4 DEP 10000

# U1 opens short at $5.00
U1 LS 5.00 1 100
U2 LB 5.00 1 100
SLEEP 500
ASSERT POSITION U1 SHORT == 100

# Price pumps sharply — short is underwater
U3 LB 20.00 1 10
U4 LS 20.00 1 10
SLEEP 300
U3 LB 20.00 1 10
U4 LS 20.00 1 10
SLEEP 500

LD 1
SLEEP 500
