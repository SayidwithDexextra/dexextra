# Test 046: Order Book Integrity After Multiple Cancels
# Place orders across multiple levels and users, cancel selectively, verify book state.
# Verifies: book integrity after selective cancellation.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 5000
U2 DEP 5000
U3 DEP 5000

U1 LB 2.00 1 30
U2 LB 2.10 1 40
U3 LB 2.20 1 50
U1 LS 3.00 1 20
U2 LS 3.10 1 30

SLEEP 300

ASSERT BID == 2.20
ASSERT ASK == 3.00

# Cancel U2's orders
U2 CA

SLEEP 300

# U2's bid at 2.10 and ask at 3.10 should be gone
ASSERT BID == 2.20
ASSERT ASK == 3.00
