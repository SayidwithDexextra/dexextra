# Test 098: Socialized Loss Haircut Persists Through Position Close
# After a haircut is applied via liquidation, closing the position should
# properly account for the haircut (proportional release).
# Verifies: haircutToConfiscate6 calculation, proportional release logic.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 200
U2 DEP 5000
U3 DEP 10000
U4 DEP 10000

# U1 opens short, U2 takes long side
U1 LS 2.50 1 100
U2 LB 2.50 1 100
SLEEP 500

# Pump price — U1 (short) is underwater
U3 LB 10.00 1 10
U4 LS 10.00 1 10
SLEEP 300
U3 LB 10.00 1 10
U4 LS 10.00 1 10
SLEEP 500

# Liquidate U1 — excess loss socialized to U2 (profitable long)
LD 1
SLEEP 500

# Now U2 closes their profitable long
U3 LS 10.00 1 100
U2 LS 10.00 1 100
SLEEP 500
