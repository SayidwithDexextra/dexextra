# Test 017: Position Netting (Close a Position)
# U1 goes long, then sells the same amount to close position.
# Verifies: position netting, PnL realization, collateral release.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 5000
U2 DEP 5000
U3 DEP 5000

# Step 1: U1 buys from U2 → U1 is long
U2 LS 2.50 1 50
U1 LB 2.50 1 50

SLEEP 500
ASSERT POSITION U1 LONG == 50

# Step 2: U1 sells back to U3 at same price → should net to zero
U3 LB 2.50 1 50
U1 LS 2.50 1 50

SLEEP 500
