# Test 025: Direct Liquidation (LD)
# Open a short position, pump the mark price, and liquidate.
# With 100% initial margin, only shorts are liquidatable (price rise blows out equity).
# Verifies: liquidation trigger, position closure via direct vault fallback.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 200
U2 DEP 5000
U3 DEP 10000
U4 DEP 10000

# U1 opens a short at $2.50
U1 LS 2.50 1 100
U2 LB 2.50 1 100
SLEEP 500
ASSERT POSITION U1 SHORT == 100

# Pump the mark by executing trades at $10
U3 LB 10.00 1 10
U4 LS 10.00 1 10
SLEEP 300
U3 LB 10.00 1 10
U4 LS 10.00 1 10
SLEEP 500

# U1's short is deeply underwater (entry $2.50, mark ~$10) — liquidate
LD 1
SLEEP 500
