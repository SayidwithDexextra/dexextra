# Test 025: Direct Liquidation (LD)
# Open a highly leveraged position, then move the mark price against it and liquidate.
# Verifies: liquidation triggers, position closure on liquidation.
# NOTE: This test may need mark price manipulation which depends on the oracle setup.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 100
U2 DEP 5000
U3 DEP 5000

# U1 takes a small-collateral long at $2.50
U2 LS 2.50 1 200
U1 LB 2.50 1 200

SLEEP 500

# U3 sells at a much lower price to crash the mark
U3 LS 0.50 1 100

SLEEP 500

# Attempt liquidation of U1
LD 1

SLEEP 500
