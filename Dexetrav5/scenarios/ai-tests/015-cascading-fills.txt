# Test 015: Cascading Fills Across Price Levels
# Stack sells at $2.00, $2.10, $2.20. Buyer sweeps through multiple levels.
# Verifies: multi-level matching, level exhaustion, partial fill at last level.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 10000
U2 DEP 5000
U3 DEP 5000
U4 DEP 10000

U1 LS 2.00 1 30
U2 LS 2.10 1 30
U3 LS 2.20 1 30

SLEEP 300

# U4 buys 70 — should fill 30@2.00 + 30@2.10 + 10@2.20
U4 LB 2.20 1 70

SLEEP 500

# 20 units remain at $2.20
ASSERT ASK == 2.20
ASSERT POSITION U4 LONG == 70
