# Test 100: Stress Test — Rapid Sequential Operations
# Rapid-fire deposits, orders, cancels, and assertions.
# Verifies: system stability under sustained load, no state corruption.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 50000
U2 DEP 50000
U3 DEP 50000
U4 DEP 50000

# Round 1: Build book
U1 LB 1.00 1 10
U1 LB 1.10 1 10
U1 LB 1.20 1 10
U1 LB 1.30 1 10
U1 LB 1.40 1 10
U2 LS 2.00 1 10
U2 LS 2.10 1 10
U2 LS 2.20 1 10
U2 LS 2.30 1 10
U2 LS 2.40 1 10

SLEEP 500
ASSERT BID == 1.40
ASSERT ASK == 2.00

# Round 2: Sweep asks
U3 LB 2.40 1 50
SLEEP 500

# Round 3: Sweep bids
U4 LS 1.00 1 50
SLEEP 500

ASSERT BOOK_EMPTY

# Round 4: New positions
U1 LB 3.00 1 100
U2 LS 3.00 1 100
SLEEP 300

ASSERT POSITION U1 LONG >= 100

# Round 5: Partial close
U3 LB 3.00 1 50
U1 LS 3.00 1 50
SLEEP 300

# Round 6: More orders
U3 LB 2.80 1 20
U3 LB 2.90 1 20
U4 LS 3.10 1 20
U4 LS 3.20 1 20
SLEEP 300

# Round 7: Cancel all for U3 and U4
U3 CA
U4 CA
SLEEP 300
ASSERT BOOK_EMPTY
