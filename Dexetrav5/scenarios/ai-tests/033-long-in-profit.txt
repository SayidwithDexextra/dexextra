# Test 033: Long Position Realizes Profit
# U1 buys at $2.00, sells at $3.00 → profit.
# Verifies: profitable long close, realized PnL positive.

STRICT ON
U1 DEP 5000
U2 DEP 5000
U3 DEP 5000

# Open long at $2.00
U2 LS 2.00 1 50
U1 LB 2.00 1 50

SLEEP 500
ASSERT POSITION U1 LONG == 50

# Close at $3.00
U3 LB 3.00 1 50
U1 LS 3.00 1 50

SLEEP 500
